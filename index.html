Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register my account?

Open the BenCare Link app, select "Register", and supply your official membership card details alongside your verified personal identification specifications.

What do I do if I didn't receive my one-time password (OTP)?

Verify that your mobile carrier signal is stable. If transmission fails repeatedly, please reach out to our hotlines or email our helpdesk to verify your account contact info.
